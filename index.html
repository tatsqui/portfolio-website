About Me

I am an aspiring full stack software developer currently located in Seattle. I am interested in collaborating with companies that promote human-centered intuitive design practices, with a focus on creating technologies that meet the needs of our varied and dynamic global world. I am passionate about nature and conservation, social justice, and socio-economic justice. I am particularly interested in Latinx and Native American communities, and increasing their participation in and access to jobs in the tech-sector, as I see this as the fastest growing sector of our economy with the lowest barriers of access.
