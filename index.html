About Me

I am currently a student at Ada Developers Academy, training to become a full stack software developer. I am currently located in Seattle. I am passionate about issues related to the environment, social justice, and socio-economic justice. I am particularly interested in advancing Latinx and Native American communities, and increasing their participation in and access to jobs in the tech-sector, as I see this as the fastest growing portion of our economy with the lowest barriers of access.
